Controls

  • Two-finger scroll pans the view on a trackpad.
  • Pinch on the trackpad to zoom the camera.
  • Use Draw/Erase for collision shapes, then Paint to stamp exact terrain sprites onto solid cells.
  • Hold Space or right-drag if you want grab-style panning.
  • Press Ctrl/Cmd+Z to undo and Shift+Ctrl/Cmd+Z to redo.
  • Press Esc in playtest to return to the editor.
